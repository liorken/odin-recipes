Description

A delicious dish that is great for dinner. Tomatoes and eggs. What more can we ask for?

Ingredients

  • 5 tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • 4 eggs
  • Paprika
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Cut the tomatoes into small pieces.
  2. Fry the tomatoes in a pan.
  3. Add the garlic gloves into the pan once the tomatoes are soft.
  4. Create holes in the tomatoe sauce and break the egggs into the holes.
